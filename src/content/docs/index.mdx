## Write for Nadikosh

Nadikosh is built by people who care about rivers — writers, researchers, devotees, scientists, and everyday citizens. This contribution studio will guide anyone through creating richly structured articles for any section of the wiki, complete with AI-assisted image suggestions, a full interactive component library, and automated series linking.
