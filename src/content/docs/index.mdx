Nadikosh is a living knowledge base dedicated to India's holy rivers.
Whether you are a first-time volunteer joining a cleanup drive, a student preparing for competitive exams, a researcher working with satellite and hydrology data,
or a devotee seeking guidance on rituals and scriptures — **this is your river.**
We are building this together, one article, one dataset, and one story at a time.
