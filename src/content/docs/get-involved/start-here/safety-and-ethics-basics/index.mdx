## What this page will cover

Every guide in Get Involved assumes you have read this page. It is not optional.

**Planned content:**

- **Personal safety on the riverbank** — terrain, water currents, flood risk, weather, and what to do in an emergency.
- **Health precautions** — exposure to polluted water, protective gear basics (gloves, boots, masks), and post-fieldwork hygiene.
- **Group safety** — buddy systems, communication protocols, and what to do if someone gets into trouble during a group drive.
- **Community ethics** — how to interact respectfully with local communities, fishermen, and residents near the river; obtaining informal consent before photographing people.
- **Legal basics** — permissions you may need before certain activities (e.g., sampling near industrial zones or reserved areas), and when to consult your NGO or local authority.
- **Environmental ethics** — leaving no trace, not disturbing flora/fauna during fieldwork, and responsible waste disposal after a cleanup.
