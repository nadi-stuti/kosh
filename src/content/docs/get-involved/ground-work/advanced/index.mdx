# 🔴 Advanced Ground Work

<Badge text="Advanced" variant="danger" />

You have substantial field experience, understand basic protocols, and are ready to lead structured, long-term river work — the kind that generates shareable data, informs policy, and builds lasting local capacity.

Guides at this level assume **programme thinking**, not just individual action.

<Aside type="note" title="Prerequisites">
Advanced guides assume comfort with Intermediate protocols. If you are stepping up from Intermediate for the first time, read [Coordinating a Group Drive](/get-involved/ground-work/intermediate/coordinating-a-group-drive/) and [Basic Water Sampling](/get-involved/ground-work/intermediate/basic-water-sampling-guide/) before proceeding.
</Aside>
