# 🟡 Intermediate Ground Work

<Badge text="Intermediate" variant="tip" />

You have field experience from multiple visits. You are comfortable on a riverbank, know the basics of waste management, and are ready to take on more structured, documented work.

Guides here involve **standardised protocols** — methods that produce comparable, shareable data or outcomes.

<Aside type="note" title="Prerequisites">
You should have completed at least 2–3 beginner-level field visits before working from this section. If you're unsure, take the [Skill Assessment Quiz](/get-involved/#skill-quiz).
</Aside>
