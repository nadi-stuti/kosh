## What this guide will cover

A standardised step-by-step protocol for conducting a litter audit on a defined river stretch in India.

**Planned content:**

- **What a litter audit is and why it matters** — difference from a general cleanup; outputs include waste-type data, density, and hotspot mapping.
- **How to define your stretch** — length, start/end markers, and GPS recording.
- **Categorisation system** — plastic types, organic waste, industrial waste, medical waste, religious offering materials, with a printable classification sheet.
- **Recording methodology** — count vs weight vs area-density methods; which to use and when.
- **Team roles** — collector, recorder, photographer.
- **Submitting your audit data** — formats compatible with Nadikosh, NGO templates, and CPCB-aligned reporting.
- **Interpreting results** — how to read your own audit data for patterns and repeat-offender waste types.
