## What this guide will cover

A practical guide for building productive working relationships with gram panchayats, urban local bodies, and temple committees for sustained river action.

**Planned content:**

- **Who the key local bodies are** — gram panchayat, nagar palika, nagar nigam, and temple trusts; their roles and responsibilities related to rivers.
- **How to make first contact** — appropriate channels, introductory framing, what to ask for and what not to ask.
- **What local bodies can provide** — waste disposal support, access permissions, manpower, and formal endorsement.
- **What volunteers can offer them** — documentation, reporting, community goodwill, and skilled labour.
- **Managing expectations** — timelines, bureaucratic realities, and how to stay useful without overcommitting.
- **Common failure modes** — what causes these partnerships to break down and how to avoid them.
