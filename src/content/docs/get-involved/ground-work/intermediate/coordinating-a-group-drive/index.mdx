## What this guide will cover

A field coordination guide for leading a group of 5–30 volunteers on a structured river drive.

**Planned content:**

- **Pre-drive checklist** — site selection, permissions, logistics, equipment sourcing, and communication to participants.
- **Briefing structure** — how to run a 10-minute pre-drive briefing that covers safety, roles, zone assignments, and protocols.
- **Zone management** — dividing a stretch into workable zones, assigning leads, and maintaining communication.
- **Managing mixed skill levels** — pairing experienced volunteers with first-timers; adapting tasks on the ground.
- **Incident protocol** — what to do if someone is injured, finds hazardous waste, or encounters aggressive behaviour.
- **Closing the drive** — waste handover, headcount, debrief structure, and documentation.
- **Post-drive reporting** — what to record, who to share it with, and how to submit to Nadikosh or your NGO.
