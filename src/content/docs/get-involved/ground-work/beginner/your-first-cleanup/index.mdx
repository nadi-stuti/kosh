## What this guide will cover

A step-by-step field guide for attending or organising your first river cleanup in India.

**Planned content:**

- **Before the cleanup** — how to find or organise a drive near you, what to confirm in advance (site, permissions, waste disposal plan), and how to prepare physically.
- **Gear checklist** — minimum required items and optional additions. Links to [What to Carry to the River](/get-involved/ground-work/beginner/what-to-carry-to-the-river/).
- **On the day** — arrival, site briefing, safe working zones, waste segregation basics, and how to stay safe.
- **During the cleanup** — how to collect, bag, and segregate waste; what to do if you encounter hazardous material (do not touch — escalate).
- **After the cleanup** — personal hygiene steps, how to record what was collected (weight, type, location), and how to report findings to the organiser or on Nadikosh.
- **Your first reflection** — a short checklist of questions to ask yourself after your first cleanup to assess readiness for Intermediate-level work.
