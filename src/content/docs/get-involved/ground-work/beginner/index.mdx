# 🟢 Beginner Ground Work

<Badge text="Beginner" variant="success" />

You have never done river fieldwork before, or you have been to one or two drives but want a proper foundation. These guides are for you.

Nothing here requires special equipment or technical training. Every guide has a clear checklist and a defined "done" state so you know when you've completed the task.

<Aside type="note" title="Before you start">
Read [Safety & Ethics Basics](/get-involved/start-here/safety-and-ethics-basics/) before your first visit to any river site.
</Aside>
