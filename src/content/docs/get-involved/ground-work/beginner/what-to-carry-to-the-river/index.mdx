## What this guide will cover

A clear, no-nonsense gear list for anyone doing beginner-level river fieldwork in India.

**Planned content:**

- **Non-negotiable essentials** — gloves, footwear, water, phone charge, ID.
- **Safety items** — basic first aid, whistle, and when a life jacket is needed.
- **Documentation basics** — notebook or phone for observations, portable charger, weather-appropriate clothing.
- **What NOT to bring** — items that are hazardous, disrespectful near sacred sites, or damaging to river ecology.
- **Seasonal variation** — how your kit changes for monsoon, summer, and winter fieldwork in different regions of India.
- **Budget gear guide** — sourcing essentials affordably in India; no imported or expensive equipment required at beginner level.
