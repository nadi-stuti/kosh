# 🌱 Get Involved

**This section is not about learning. It is about doing.**

If you want to understand *why* rivers are polluted or *how* pollution is measured, head to [Pollution Academy](/pollution-academy/). Come back here when you are ready to act.

Everything in this section is built around a single question: **what is your next action?**

<Aside type="note" title="India-focused">
All guides, tools, protocols, and contacts in this section are specific to **India and Indian rivers**. Global models have inspired some of this work, but every recommendation here is grounded in the Indian context.
</Aside>

---

## How to use this section

<Steps>

1. **Take the skill assessment** below to find your level — beginner, intermediate, or advanced.

2. **Pick your path** — ground work, digital tools, event organising, or advocacy.

3. **Act.** Each article gives you a clear goal, a checklist, and a next step.

</Steps>
