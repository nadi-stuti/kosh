## What this guide will cover

A practical action guide for using the CPCB's online water quality monitoring dashboard to find real-time and historical data for Indian rivers.

**Planned content:**

- **Finding your station** — how to locate monitoring stations on your river or in your district using the map or search interface.
- **Reading the dashboard** — what each indicator panel shows; how to interpret colour-coded status (DO, BOD, pH, etc.) at a glance.
- **Viewing historical trends** — how to pull up time-series data for a station and read basic trend direction.
- **Downloading data** — steps to export station data for use in your own reports or submissions.
- **Understanding data gaps** — why some stations show no data, what "offline" means, and how to work around missing readings.
- **Using CPCB data in complaints** — how to reference official CPCB readings when filing a pollution complaint (links to [Advocacy & Policy → How to File a Complaint](/get-involved/advocacy-and-policy/how-to-file-a-pollution-complaint/)).
- **Known issues** — interface quirks and workarounds current as of last verification date.
