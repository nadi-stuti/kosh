# 🖥️ Digital Tools

This section tells you **how to use** the digital tools relevant to river work in India.

It does not explain what these tools are or the science behind them. If you need that foundation, visit [Pollution Academy → Tools & Tech](/pollution-academy/tools-and-tech/). Come back here when you are ready to open the tool and do something with it.

<Aside type="danger" title="Most-maintained section in Nadikosh">
Tool interfaces, portal URLs, and app versions change frequently. Every guide here carries a **"Last verified on [date]"** tag. If something looks different on your screen, use the Giscus comment thread at the bottom of that article to flag it. We will update as soon as possible.
</Aside>
