## What this guide will cover

A task-by-task action guide for using [India-WRIS](https://indiawris.gov.in/wris/) — India's national water resources information portal — for practical river work.

**Planned content:**

- **Getting oriented** — how the portal is structured; the difference between the map interface, reports, and data downloads.
- **Task: Find your river zone** — step-by-step: how to navigate to your basin, select your river, and view its extent on the map.
- **Task: Read river and water quality layers** — how to turn on monitoring station layers, read their status indicators, and understand what the colour codes mean.
- **Task: Export a basin report** — how to generate and download a summary report for a specific sub-basin or district.
- **Task: Find dam and reservoir data** — relevant for understanding upstream flow control affecting your stretch.
- **Task: Use the telemetry module** — how to read real-time flow and level data where available.
- **Known limitations** — data gaps, update frequencies, and what WRIS does not cover (and where to go instead).
