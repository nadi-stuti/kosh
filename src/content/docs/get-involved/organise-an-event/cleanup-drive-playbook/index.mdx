## What this playbook will cover

A complete, repeatable action plan for organising a river cleanup drive in India — from the first idea to final waste disposal.

**Planned content:**

<Steps>
1. **Site selection** — how to choose a suitable stretch; access, safety, waste type, and community sensitivity checks.
2. **Permissions and registrations** — when you need prior permission, who to contact (local body, forest department, river authority), and what to carry on the day.
3. **Logistics planning** — equipment sourcing (bags, gloves, tools), waste disposal coordination with local municipal body, transport, and headcount management.
4. **Volunteer briefing** — how to run a pre-drive briefing covering safety, zones, roles, and waste segregation protocol.
5. **Running the drive** — zone management, incident response, documentation during the event.
6. **Closing the drive** — waste handover process, headcount, debrief, and on-site photography checklist.
7. **Post-drive reporting** — data to record, formats to use, and how to submit to Nadikosh or your NGO partner.
</Steps>
