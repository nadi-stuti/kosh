## What this playbook will cover

A step-by-step plan for organising a river awareness walk — one of the lowest-barrier, highest-visibility river actions available to any community group.

**Planned content:**

- **Defining your goal** — awareness of a specific issue (e.g., encroachment, industrial discharge) vs general public education; how the goal shapes everything else.
- **Route planning** — how to choose a walk route that is visually impactful, safe, accessible, and legally permissible.
- **Messaging and materials** — what to communicate, how to create simple banners or placards, and what to avoid saying publicly without verified data.
- **Involving local communities** — how to invite and brief local residents, shopkeepers, and religious community leaders along the route.
- **Media and documentation** — how to inform local press, live-post responsibly, and document the walk for your archive.
- **Permissions** — when a public gathering requires prior notice to local authorities in India, and how to file it.
- **Post-walk follow-up** — sustaining engagement after the walk; how to convert participants into regular volunteers.
