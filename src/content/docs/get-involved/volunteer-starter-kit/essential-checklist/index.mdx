## What this checklist will cover

A single, printable pre-field checklist that confirms a new volunteer is ready for their first river visit — across gear, knowledge, contacts, and personal safety.

**Planned content:**

- **Gear confirmed** — gloves, footwear, water, phone charge, ID, basic first aid. Links to [What to Carry to the River](/get-involved/ground-work/beginner/what-to-carry-to-the-river/) for the full gear guide.
- **Safety basics read** — confirm you have read [Safety & Ethics Basics](/get-involved/start-here/safety-and-ethics-basics/).
- **Emergency contacts saved** — local hospital number, your organiser's number, and one backup contact who knows you are at the river.
- **Site confirmed** — you know the exact location, how to get there, and who is leading the session.
- **Your role confirmed** — you know what you are doing on the day (collection, documentation, coordination) and have been briefed.
- **Weather checked** — especially critical for monsoon visits; site-specific flood and current risk awareness.
- **Post-visit plan confirmed** — you know where waste goes after the drive, and what data to submit and to whom.
