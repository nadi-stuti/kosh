## What this guide will cover

A short, practical guide that helps a new volunteer locate their nearest major river zone in the [Our Rivers](/our-rivers/) section and find existing local action and community groups.

**Planned content:**

- **India's 9 river zones on one map** — a simple visual overview of the Ganga, Yamuna, Sindhu, Brahmaputra, Godavari, Narmada, Mahanadi, Krishna, and Kaveri zones and which states and districts fall within each.
- **How to find your zone** — a district-to-zone lookup (searchable table or simple map interaction) so you can identify your zone in under a minute.
- **What to look for in your zone page** — once you land on a zone page in Our Rivers, this guide explains which sections are most useful for a new volunteer: health snapshot, community & action, and local organisations.
- **What to do if your river is not listed yet** — Our Rivers is growing; if your specific river has no page yet, this guide suggests interim options (WRIS, local NGO search, Thinking Grounds).
- **Next step** — once you know your zone and your nearest active group, head to [Your First Cleanup](/get-involved/ground-work/beginner/your-first-cleanup/) or contact the listed organisation directly.
