# 📢 Advocacy & Policy

Some river problems cannot be solved by a cleanup drive. They require you to engage the system — file a complaint, talk to an official, work with an NGO, or speak to the media.

Every guide here tells you **exactly what to do** in each of those situations. No theory. No history of Indian water law. Just the steps.

<Aside type="note" title="India-specific, ground-level focus">
All guides here are specific to India's administrative and legal context. Contacts, portals, and processes are targeted at the district and state level — the level where most river advocates actually operate.
</Aside>
