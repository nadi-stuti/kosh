# NGOs and Ashrams Working for Ganga

## NGOs

- **Ganga Action Parivar (GAP)**: Founded by Swami Gyan Swaroop Sanand, this NGO focuses on cleaning and conserving the Ganga River through community involvement and advocacy.
- **Sankat Mochan Foundation**: Based in Varanasi, it works on environmental education, pollution control, and sustainable development along the Ganga.
- **Tarun Bharat Sangh**: Led by Rajendra Singh, this organization promotes water conservation and community-based river management in the Ganga basin.
- **WWF India**: Works on biodiversity conservation, including protecting aquatic life in the Ganga and reducing pollution.
- **Greenpeace India**: Campaigns for clean rivers, including the Ganga, through awareness and policy advocacy.
- **Ganga Seva Nidhi**: Dedicated to the service and protection of the Ganga through various initiatives like tree planting and waste management.

## Ashrams

- **Parmarth Niketan Ashram**: Located in Rishikesh, this ashram conducts daily Ganga Aarti and runs programs for river cleaning and spiritual education.
- **Chitrakoot Ashram**: Focuses on environmental conservation and spiritual practices along the Ganga.
- **Bharat Mata Mandir Ashram**: In Haridwar, it promotes Ganga conservation through religious and community activities.
