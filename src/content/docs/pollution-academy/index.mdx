Rivers don't pollute themselves. Understanding **why**, **how**, and **who** is behind India's river pollution crisis is the first step toward fixing it. Pollution Academy is Nadikosh's educational core — a growing library of structured articles, tool guides, and case studies built for everyone from first-time volunteers to policy researchers.

<Aside type="tip" title="New here?">
Start with **Why Rivers Are Polluted** — our 27-part flagship series that walks you through the full picture, step by step.
</Aside>

## How to use Pollution Academy

<Steps>
1. **Pick your path** — Visit [Reading Paths](/pollution-academy/reading-paths/) if you are unsure where to start.
2. **Follow the series** — Work through *Why Rivers Are Polluted* in order for the fullest understanding.
3. **Go deeper** — Use *How Pollution Is Measured*, *Tools & Tech*, and *Case Studies* as reference when you need more detail.
4. **Take action** — Once you have the foundation, head to [Get Involved](/get-involved/) to apply what you have learned.
</Steps>
